About

Spotify Explorer is one of my weekend projects, which I use to learn more about Angular (6) and Angular Material, and to apply the "human-computer interaction" principles that I learned at the university to a real project. It is built using Angular 6, Angular Material and other helper libraries. The user is required to have a Spotify account and needs to log in to Spotify before he/she can use the app. It has 2 major features: Library and Search, which will be explained in the following paragraphs.

In the Library feature, user can explore his/her Spotify library, including playlists, songs, albums and artists. The playlists which he/she owns or follows will be listed in the Playlists section. Songs, albums and artists that are saved to the user's library can be seen in the Songs, Albums and Artists section respectively. A preview of a song (if available) can be played by clicking the particular song, also the detail of the playlists, albums or artists can be seen by clicking the particular item.

In the Search feature, the user can search through Spotify's database to search for artists, songs, albums and playlists. A preview of a song (if available) can also be played by clicking the particular song. Once the user finds the item that he/she desires, he/she can save it to his/her library by clicking the follow icon favorite_border (for artists, albums and playlists) or the vertical menu more_vert (for songs).
